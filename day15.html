Vice Chancellor's Message

Welcome to the University of Chitral-a new established public sector university in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. Our mission goes beyond simply classroom teaching and basic research-we strive to provide an academic environment that encourages dialogue, critical thinking, innovation and respect for diversity and inclusiveness. Our faculty is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement-bringing real-world experience into the classroom.
